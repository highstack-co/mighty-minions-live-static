Frequently Asked Questions

For more information on our project, check out our WHITEPAPER
What is Mighty Minions?

Mighty Minions NFTs are a collection of super cute 7,777 NFTs on the Solana blockchain that serve as an access token to our Coliseum, Training Grounds, and PVP Arena. Each Minion is uniquely hand-drawn and belongs to one of three faction classes; Fire, Water, & Forest.
Why should I own a Mighty Minions NFT?

Besides the fact that they are clearly the cutest NFTs out there [in our humble opinion], they can be used to play/compete in our weekly tournaments, quests, PVP arena, and training grounds where one can level up their Mighty Minions, win rewards, and gamble against their fellow community members. As far as we know, we're the first in the entire crypto space to create a gambling game where the NFTs determine the "betting" odds. Oh and did I forget to mention that ALL Mighty Minion NFT hodlers will have a chance to win Faction rewards in SOL every week?!
How much is a Mighty Minion NFT?

Phase One sold out at 1 SOL a minion. Phase Two mint will start at 1.2 SOL.
Will there be a pre-sale?

Yes, to join the pre-sale you must earn a whitelist spot. There will be a maximum cap of 999 whitelist spots given out for Phase 2.
Wen is mint date?

We sold out our Phase One mint! We just recently opened up Phase Two whitelist spots! Join our Discord to see how you can earn a spot!
What kind of game will this be?

It will be a gambling game of chance and strategy where you battle others using your Mighty Minions to win rewards, EXP, and prestige. The odds of winning battles will be determined by your Mighty Minion’s level, stats, and faction.
Do I need to gamble to level up my Mighty Minion?

No, you may play for free and level up your Mighty Minion. Apart from gameplay, participating and engaging in our community events will result in huge in-game EXP bonuses as well.

Do I need to gamble to play the game?

No, all Mighty Minion NFTs staked will receive $MTK tokens daily that can be redeemed and used to play and train for free. Apart from the gameplay, participating and engaging in our community events will result in huge in-game EXP bonuses.
Will leveling up my Mighty Minions NFT increase its value?

Yes, the more you level up your Minion, the more valuable it becomes. Think of it as growing your asset to produce more cash flow. As your Minion levels up, it’s chance of winning more battles, tournaments, and quests increases. Additionally, reaching certain levels will give you access to special NFT airdrops [Reach level 10 for Mini-Monster Airdrops].
Do I earn passive income just for hodling a Mighty Minion NFT?

Yes, there are a ton of ways to earn passive income through Mighty Minion NFTs. It's what makes owning and hodling one so great.
‍
1. Passive royalties from Coliseum.
2. Staked Mighty Minions will be airdropped $MTK
3. Owners of Mighty Minions will be awarded NFT airdrops for future drops/collections.
4. All Mighty Minion NFTs will be automatically entered into free weekly tournaments where one can earn $SOL by placing in the top 8.
5. Win the a tournament and earn "Hall of Fame" status, and receive Mighty Minion Trophy NFTs which yield additional royalties.
6. Every week, there will be one winning Faction. If you hold a Minion from the winning faction, you will be paid SOL.
